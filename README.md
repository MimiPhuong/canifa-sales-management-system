# CANIFA Sales Management System

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing and designing a **Sales Management System for CANIFA**, aiming to improve business operations such as order processing, inventory tracking, and payment management.

The system addresses common issues in retail operations, including manual workflows, lack of real-time data, and inconsistent information across departments.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Through stakeholder analysis, several key issues were identified:

- Manual order processing causing delays and errors  
- Inventory not updated in real-time  
- Payment status not synchronized across departments  
- Difficulty in tracking returns and order status  

---

## 👨‍💼 My Role

**Business Analyst**

---

## 🔍 Key Responsibilities

- Conducted stakeholder interviews (Sales, Warehouse, Accounting) to gather business requirements  
- Analyzed current business processes and identified inefficiencies  
- Defined and documented **functional and non-functional requirements**  
- Designed **business workflows (As-Is & To-Be)** to improve operational efficiency  
- Developed system models including:
  - Use Case Diagrams  
  - Data Flow Diagrams (DFD)  
  - Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD)  
- Collaborated with team members to align business needs with system design  

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

The system provides the following core functionalities:

- Product & Inventory Management  
- Order Processing & Tracking  
- Payment Management  
- Customer Management  
- Reporting & Analytics
